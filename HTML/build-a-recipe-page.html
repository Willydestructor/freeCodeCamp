Cheesecake

Traditional spanish cheesecake

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Cookies
  • Butter
  • Mascarpone cheese

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter in the microwave
  2. Crush the cookies in a bowl
  3. Mix the melted butter with the crushed cookies
  4. Spray the mix in the container and let it rest
  5. Mix all the other ingredients and pour them on the buttered cookies
  6. Preheat the oven
  7. Bake everything and let it rest for 10 min
